Why are we Doing This?

  • Sustainability: making sure the needs of the present do not outweigh the needs of the future
  • Contextualize the planet-warming potential of the Association
  • Make sure our money serves a livable climate
  • Get in compliance with campus climate goals:
    • Revising Climate Action Plan for first time in a decade
  • Goal: Become first carbon-neutral student government in the country
    • First-of-its-kind initiative among UC student governments

What this Audit is Not

  • An inquisition
  • A forum to air grievances
  • Marching orders
    • Solutions will be implemented in a collaborative, deliberative manner

How we did it

  • All financial details implemented in Supabase, a data management system
  • Use the Climatiq API
  • Two-step process:
    1. Feed purchase description into system, which matches it to an emissions factor (i.e., beef jerky → consumer_goods-type_snack_foods)
    2. System calculates \(\text{CO}_2\) equivalent per purchase
  • Use Python/R for visualizations and statistical analysis, with live data fetch from Supabase

Contextualizing the Carbon

kg CO₂e total emissions

  • Equivalent to driving a gas-powered car roughly 730,000 miles
  • Same as the annual electricity use of around 40 U.S. homes
  • Would take about 286 acres of U.S. forests a full year to sequester
  • 417 round trips from SD to NY
  • Roughly 23,000,000 smartphones charged
    • Source: EPA Greenhouse Gas Equivalencies Calculator

Takeaways

  • Travel & Transportation and Professional Services dominate totals at VP External and Transportation & Transformation.
  • ASCE has the largest and most dispersed per-transaction distribution — many small Events and Equipment rentals plus a long outlier tail.
  • Equipment & Rentals is the largest single category by transaction count.
  • Negative / zero co2e rows (credits, reversals, unmapped) were excluded from the log-scale view and shown as Uncategorized in totals.
